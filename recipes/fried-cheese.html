Fried Cheese

Description

Experience the irresistible crunch and gooey goodness of our fried cheese. Melted to perfection, each golden bite delivers a savory explosion that is both comforting and indulgent. A crispy exterior gives way to a molten center, creating a mouthwatering symphony of textures. Perfect as a snack or appetizer, our fried cheese is a delightful treat for cheese enthusiasts and casual food lovers alike.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Oil for frying
  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional: Fruit compote or topping

Steps

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter for the crust.
  2. Preheat the oven.
  3. Blend cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Pour the mixture over the crust and bake.
  5. Cool in the oven, then refrigerate.
  6. Add toppings if desired, then slice and enjoy!
