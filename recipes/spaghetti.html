Spaghetti

Description

Savor the simplicity of our spaghetti – perfectly cooked noodles enveloped in a rich tomato sauce infused with garlic, onions, and savory Italian seasonings. Optional meat additions provide a hearty touch, while a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese crowns this classic dish. Quick to prepare, yet endlessly satisfying, our spaghetti promises a delightful journey of flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or meatballs (optional)
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water and cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, sauté garlic and onions in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add ground beef or meatballs (optional) and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.
  6. Drain the cooked spaghetti and toss it with the prepared sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
